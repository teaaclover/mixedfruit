Recipe | Instruction

Looking for a refeshing treat for summer or anytime? This tasty dessert made with simple ingredients won't dissappoint you. Check out the recipe below!

1/ Ingredients

  • - 1 ripe mangos
  • - tapiocal pearls or chia seeds (you can pick one depending on your preference!)
  • - coconut milk
  • - 1 tbsp sugar (optional)

2/ Preparation

  • 1. Start off with cutting fresh mangos into small square pieces.
  • 2. Then in a pot adds coconut milk, sugar, tapiocal pearls and brings them to a simmer.
  • 3. In a bowl or jar, add all the ingredients and serve chill!
